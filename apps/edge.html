About Microsoft Edge for Business

Microsoft Edge for Business is a secure, enterprise-grade browser purpose-built to work with Microsoft 365. It integrates with Entra ID, enforces policies through Intune and surfaces Copilot, Teams, Outlook and SharePoint features natively in the browser — separating work browsing from personal browsing with a dedicated work profile.

It's best used as the everyday browser for any M365-licensed workforce, where speed, security, productivity tooling and AI-assisted research matter. IT, security, compliance and procurement teams benefit from centralised management; end users benefit from faster SharePoint access, integrated Copilot and built-in PDF tooling. Edge solves the common enterprise problem of employees using a patchwork of personal browsers that leak corporate data and miss M365 integrations — replacing it with a single, governable, AI-aware browsing experience.
