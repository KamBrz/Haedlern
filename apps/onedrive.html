About OneDrive

Microsoft OneDrive is your personal cloud home in Microsoft 365 — a secure, always-available file store that syncs across every device, handles versioning automatically and keeps documents accessible offline. It's the backbone for personal work files, attachments in Outlook, and any file shared from a 1:1 Teams chat.

It's best used for any work file that doesn't yet belong to a team — drafts, templates, personal reference documents, attachments from email — with selective sync, sharing links and restore points built in. Every employee benefits, but especially mobile workers, knowledge workers and anyone who previously relied on local drives or USB sticks. OneDrive solves the everyday problem of files being lost, out of date, or trapped on a single device — replacing it with a secure, backed-up, shareable workspace that follows the person, not the machine.
