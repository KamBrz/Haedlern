About Power BI

Microsoft Power BI is M365's business-intelligence platform — a tool for pulling data from dozens of sources, modelling it and publishing interactive dashboards that stay live and governed. It covers everything from quick personal reports to enterprise-scale data models, with row-level security, scheduled refresh and AI-assisted insights.

It's best used for KPI dashboards, executive reporting, finance and operations reporting, sales analytics and any recurring report currently rebuilt in Excel every month. Analysts, finance teams, operations, sales leadership and executives benefit most, with IT owning the underlying data infrastructure. Power BI solves the perennial business-reporting problem of numbers taking days to produce and being out of date by the time they arrive — replacing it with always-on dashboards everyone trusts.
