About Whiteboard

Microsoft Whiteboard is M365's infinite digital canvas for collaborative thinking — a visual space for brainstorms, retrospectives, workshops, design sprints and live meeting co-creation. It works in Teams meetings, on touch devices and in the browser, with templates, sticky notes, inking, reactions and real-time co-authoring.

It's best used for workshops, brainstorming sessions, retrospectives, design thinking, planning sessions and any meeting where "whiteboard in the room" is part of the pattern. Facilitators, agile teams, product teams, strategy, HR, L&D and design teams benefit most. Whiteboard solves the classic hybrid-working problem of in-person staff gathering around a whiteboard while remote colleagues watch from a tiny camera feed — by giving everyone, everywhere, the same canvas, inputs and visibility.
