About Word

Microsoft Word is M365's document platform — a writing, formatting and collaboration tool that combines decades of professional-publishing depth with modern co-authoring, Editor, Designer and Copilot features. It's far more than a typewriter replacement; it's where most written business output is created, reviewed and signed off.

It's best used for reports, contracts, proposals, policies, templates, bids and any long-form document that needs structure, consistent branding and collaborative review. HR, legal, L&D, marketing, bid teams, consultants and leadership benefit most from disciplined Word practice. Word solves the everyday problem of documents that are inconsistent in look, painful to reformat and slow to review — by combining styles, templates, track changes, co-authoring and Copilot into a faster, cleaner way to produce documents that hold together at scale.
