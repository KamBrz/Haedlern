About Excel

Microsoft Excel remains the backbone of business data work — a spreadsheet platform that now spans formulas, dynamic arrays, Power Query, PivotTables, Power Pivot and Copilot-powered natural-language analysis. Modern Excel is far more than a grid of numbers; it's a self-service data tool capable of pulling from dozens of sources, shaping data at scale and producing interactive reports.

It's ideal for financial modelling, budgeting, operational reporting, data cleaning, forecasting and any ad-hoc analysis that doesn't warrant a full BI project. Finance, operations, HR, analysts, project managers and department heads benefit most. Excel solves the universal business problem of data being scattered across systems and manipulated inconsistently — by giving everyone a single, familiar environment to transform, analyse and share numbers, without waiting on a central data team.
