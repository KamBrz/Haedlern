About Copilot across M365

Copilot across M365 is Microsoft's AI layer embedded directly inside the apps your teams already use every day — Teams, Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, Loop and more. Instead of switching to a separate tool, users get context-aware AI built into the exact place they're working, grounded in their own files, emails, chats and meetings.

It's best used for the high-frequency, small-but-repetitive tasks that eat up the working day: summarising meetings and email threads, drafting responses, turning Word docs into slides, analysing Excel data in plain English, and catching up on missed Teams chats. Every knowledge worker benefits, but the biggest time-back wins typically land with managers, executives, project leads and anyone whose calendar is meeting-heavy. It solves the problem M365 has always had — powerful features hidden behind menus no one clicks — by letting people simply describe what they want in natural language.
