About Outlook

Microsoft Outlook is M365's email and calendar hub — now powered by Copilot, with deep integrations into Teams, To Do and SharePoint. Modern Outlook is far more than an inbox; it's where most people manage their day, triage communication, schedule meetings and turn email into action.

It's best used for disciplined inbox management, calendar scheduling, meeting prep, delegate access and group mailbox handling — especially when combined with Copilot's summarisation and drafting. Every employee benefits, but managers, executives, executive assistants, sales and customer-facing teams gain the most from structured Outlook practice. Outlook solves the eternal knowledge-worker problem of drowning in email — with rules, categories, quick steps, Copilot summaries and calendar-blocking turning a cluttered inbox into a functional, time-respecting command centre.
