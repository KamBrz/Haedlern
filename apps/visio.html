About Visio on the Web

Microsoft Visio on the Web is M365's browser-based diagramming tool — a lightweight but capable way to build flowcharts, process diagrams, org charts, network diagrams and basic floor plans directly in the browser, with diagrams stored in OneDrive or SharePoint and embeddable in Teams and Loop.

It's best used for mapping business processes, documenting workflows, designing org structures, planning events or floor layouts, and visualising any system that's easier to understand as a diagram than as text. Operations, process improvement, IT, HR, facilities, project managers and business analysts benefit most. Visio solves the common problem of important processes being described only in long, tangled paragraphs of text — by giving the business a consistent, version-controlled visual language for how work actually flows.
