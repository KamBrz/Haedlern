About the M365 Copilot App

The M365 Copilot App is Microsoft's standalone AI hub — a dedicated home for Copilot that lives alongside the rest of Microsoft 365. It's where people go to start a prompt, browse AI-generated suggestions, search across their organisational knowledge, work with shared agents and kick off Pages for collaborative drafting, all in one place.

It's best suited to daily knowledge work: drafting, summarising, researching, preparing for meetings, pulling information out of long documents and quickly building first-pass deliverables. Every role benefits, but the biggest gains tend to land with knowledge workers, managers, executives, analysts and anyone spending significant time writing, reading or searching. It solves the most common Copilot-adoption problem — people know Copilot exists but don't have a consistent place to use it or a clear sense of what to ask — by giving them a single, obvious starting point and a repeatable daily-AI habit.
