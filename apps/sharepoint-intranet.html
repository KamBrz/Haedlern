About SharePoint Intranet

SharePoint Intranet is the communication layer of M365 — a platform for building modern intranet sites, hub sites and the Viva Connections employee experience. Pages can be designed without code, personalised per audience, and surfaced inside Teams so employees get a consistent internal web experience wherever they work.

It's best used for company intranets, department hubs, leadership blogs, policy centres, news feeds, onboarding portals and any communication that needs to reach all employees consistently. Internal comms, HR, L&D, leadership and communications teams benefit most. SharePoint Intranet solves the common problem of an outdated, ignored corporate intranet — by giving comms teams a fast, modern, mobile-ready way to publish content that people actually read, and that brings together M365 content the workforce is already engaging with.
