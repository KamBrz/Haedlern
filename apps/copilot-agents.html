About M365 Copilot Agents

M365 Copilot Agents are custom AI assistants you build on top of Microsoft 365 Copilot to automate specific business processes, answer role-specific questions and connect to your organisation's own data. Unlike the default Copilot experience, agents can be scoped to a team, grounded in specific SharePoint content or line-of-business systems, and triggered inside the apps your people already use.

They're ideal for HR helpdesks, IT support triage, onboarding assistants, sales playbook bots, policy lookup tools and any repetitive knowledge-work where the answer "already exists somewhere but no one can find it". Best suited to IT, operations, HR, knowledge management and process owners who want to scale expertise without scaling headcount. Agents solve the classic M365 problem of valuable content being locked in fragmented sources — turning scattered documents into a single, conversational answer.
