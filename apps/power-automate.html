About Power Automate

Microsoft Power Automate is M365's low-code automation platform — a workflow tool for connecting apps, automating approvals and replacing repetitive manual work with triggered flows. It talks to hundreds of services (M365, Dynamics, SharePoint, SQL, Salesforce, ServiceNow) and supports both cloud flows and desktop/RPA automation.

It's best used for approval routing, form-to-email workflows, document generation, data moves between systems, and any task that has an obvious pattern and happens at least weekly. Operations, HR, finance, IT, customer service and anyone whose work involves "copy this there, email that, then update a list" benefit most. Power Automate solves the hidden productivity tax of manual, repeatable admin — letting the business automate it themselves without opening a ticket with IT or waiting for a developer.
