About PowerPoint

Microsoft PowerPoint is the standard for business presentations — now enhanced with Designer for automatic layouts, Presenter Coach for rehearsal and Copilot for generating entire decks from a prompt. Modern PowerPoint is far more than bullet lists on slides; it's a storytelling, design and live-presenting platform.

It's best used for leadership updates, sales pitches, internal training, town halls, client-facing proposals and any story that benefits from visual structure. Leadership, sales, marketing, L&D, HR and anyone presenting regularly benefits most. PowerPoint solves the widespread problem of decks that are slow to build, inconsistent in brand, and painful to present — by combining templates, design intelligence and Copilot into a faster, more polished way to build and deliver presentations that actually land.
