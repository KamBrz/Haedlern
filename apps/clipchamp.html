About Clipchamp (Stream)

Clipchamp is Microsoft 365's built-in video editor — a browser-based tool for recording, editing and sharing professional video content without specialist software or a steep learning curve. It replaces the older Stream Classic experience and sits alongside OneDrive and SharePoint, so exports stay within your secure M365 environment.

It's ideal for internal communications, training videos, leadership updates, customer-facing demos and polished highlight clips from Teams meeting recordings. Teams most likely to benefit: internal comms, L&D, marketing, sales enablement and team leads who record their own screen captures. It solves the common problem of messy, inconsistent video output produced with whatever tool people can find — replacing it with a single, governed, on-brand way to create video that anyone can pick up in minutes.
