01 Discover Week 0 · Free 30-min call

We listen before we pitch.

Before we quote a single number or build a single slide, we get to know your business. In a free 30-minute discovery call we learn how your teams actually use Microsoft 365 today — what's landed, what's been ignored, and where the friction is quietly costing you hours every week.

  • Review your current M365 stack, licensing and existing adoption

02 Customise Weeks 1–2 · Programme design

Built around your people, not a template.

Off-the-shelf training is what most providers sell. We don't. Every session is purpose-built around your industry, your apps and your people's real skill levels. We design the agenda, the scenarios and the example files using your actual business context — so nothing feels abstract.

  • Agenda tailored to your team's apps, roles and experience
  • Real-world scenarios from your industry, not generic demos
  • Pre-session skills survey so we land at exactly the right level
What you leave with: a training plan signed off by you — with agenda, outcomes and success measures agreed upfront.
03 Deliver Session day & follow-up

Practical skills they can use tomorrow.

Our trainers don't read slides — they run live, hands-on sessions inside Microsoft Teams that bring the content to life. Attendees leave with skills they can apply the very next morning, plus a recording, a completion certificate and a post-session ROI report so you can show leadership exactly what changed.

  • Live, interactive sessions delivered via Microsoft Teams
  • 24-hour session recording and completion certificates for every attendee
  • Post-session ROI report measuring skills gained and time saved
What you leave with: measurable capability uplift in your team, with evidence you can share with leadership and finance.
